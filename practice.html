Civil Service Exam Practice Test

Section 1: Verbal Ability - Sentence Completion

1. She walked ______ the street and stopped ______ the coffee shop to meet her friend.

  • A) down, by
  • B) across, to
  • C) along, at
  • D) through, in
  • E) over, for

2. By the time we arrived at the station, the train _______.

  • A) leaves
  • B) had left
  • C) was leaving
  • D) left
  • E) has left

3. Neither the teacher nor the students _______ ready for the meeting.

  • A) is
  • B) are
  • C) were
  • D) be
  • E) am

4. Let us consider the _______ that this new law will have on the lives of our citizens.

  • A) affected
  • B) affect
  • C) impact
  • D) results
  • E) effects

5. They are both excellent books, well written and encompassing, but the first book is _______.

  • A) better
  • B) the best
  • C) more good
  • D) also best
  • E) better of both

6. By the time Maria arrived at the party, all the cake _______.

  • A) had ate
  • B) was eaten
  • C) had eaten
  • D) had been eaten
  • E) were being eaten

7. _______ is a great way to stay healthy and energized.

  • A) Run
  • B) To run
  • C) Runs
  • D) Ran
  • E) Running

8. The illness that she is experiencing is brought on ______ a virus.

  • A) to
  • B) by
  • C) with
  • D) from
  • E) about

9. Despite his initial hesitation, he finally _______ the proposal after much persuasion from his colleagues.

  • A) gave away
  • B) gave off
  • C) gave in to
  • D) gave out
  • E) gave up

10. If they _______ the instructions carefully, they _______ made fewer mistakes.

  • A) followed, would have
  • B) have followed, would have
  • C) followed, will have
  • D) had followed, would have
  • E) follow, will
